I would prefer to train in private. Do you offer one-on-one personal training?

Yes. We do offer group training classes, but we also offer one-on-one personal training for those who would prefer privacy and extra attention from their trainer. Our private training includes nutrition planning, workouts that you can do at home, progress reports, accountability checks, and more. We find private training to be beneficial because it allows you to form a personal relationship with your trainer. We treat all of our new clients as partners; your goals become ours.

What has changed?

During this time we are able to social distance with 1800 ft.² we have plenty of room for one on one training to keep our distance and keep everything clean. We ask every client to wash or sanitize their hands after entering the building as well as before they leave. As we know handwashing is one of the biggest defenses against this disease. We have plenty of hand sanitizer for your use. All equipment is disinfected in between each and every training session. Group trainings are limited to 4 individuals. Each having a 12-foot space in between each person to work out in.


Do you have student discount?

Yes, we offer discounted student rates at $30/class. This rate is applicable for walk-in only at select class times* and available for undergrad and graduate students with a valid college ID upon check-in at the front desk. Hours: Monday through Friday, applicable to all classes between 8:00am & 4:30pm.


Can you create a personalized weight loss plan for my body?

Yes, this is our specialty. We are body transformation experts because we know how to help you assess your body’s needs as well as how your body responds to different movements and foods. Do you want a weight loss program that is tailored specifically to you? Contact us for a consultation and find out how Sault Fitness can create a fitness and nutrition plan based on your body and your goals.


How long are tainig sessions? May I train more than once a day?

Sessions are approximately one hour, including The Warm Up and stretch/cool down. We do limit training to one (1) class/day to allow your body to rest and recover properly.


Are cell phones, photos, videos allowed during class?

No. Out of respect for your teammates’ privacy and to ensure that your Coaches have your undivided attention, please refrain from cell phone usage, photos, or videos while training. We do encourage you to take team pics and other reasonable photos after class and share them socially!


Is there a minimum age limit?

You must be at least 18 years of age to hit The Turf. Clients under the age of 18 may be considered on a case-by-case basis and must have a legal parent or guardian present upon check-in.


Do I need a personal trainer?

At REBEL we will be able to identify strengths and weaknesses throughout your movement patterns and posture. Creating a routine to not only meet your goals but better your overall functionality and mobility to help you in everyday life.
